About Us

"Our goal is to replace traditional parents' ideas and beliefs with modern ideas based on scientific studies and successful experiences"

Our project seeks to make a system that make the communication between the parents and their children easy. we do that by using natural language processing to support the parents by modern studies to build the morals of their children. so we search for data, situations and see how parents deal with them at the end we came with our system with these features

  • a chat bot that answers the parents’ questions.
  • parents are able to share their thoughts with each other.
  • parents can communicate directly with specialists to have their questions answered
  • We have a chat bot which will make it easy for parents to get the information
